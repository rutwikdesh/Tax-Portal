About Us

Memorial, an NGO for poor child education,directly benefitting over 35,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 900 live welfare projects on education, livelihood and women empowerment.

Education is both the means as well as the end to a better life: the means because it empowers an individual to earn his/her livelihood and the end. Doubtless, education is the most powerful catalyst for social transformation. But child education cannot be done in isolation. A child will go to school only if the family, particularly the mother, is assured of healthcare and empowered. Moreover, when an elder sibling is relevantly skilled to be employable and begins earning, the journey of empowerment continues beyond the present generation.
